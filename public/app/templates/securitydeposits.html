Security Deposits

A Host may, at the Host’s discretion, choose to collect a security deposit from a Guest who reserves Spaces as set out in the relevant Space Details (“Security Deposit”). As a Guest, you are required to pay any Security Deposit required by a Host before you may use the applicable Space in accordance with the terms of the relevant Space Details. Such payments will be arranged directly between the Host and the Guest without being processed by Venuse. As a Host, you must disclose any Security Deposit associated with a Space in the relevant Space Details. Except to the extent that the Host has incurred costs as a result of the Guest’s occupation of the Space to put the Space back into the condition it was in prior to being licensed to the Guest, or as otherwise set out in the relevant Space Details, the Host will refund in full the Security Deposit after the Guest’s license to use the Space has ended. Security Deposits for Venuse Concierge Services as well as the applicable terms and conditions are vendor-specific and shall be provided at the time the request for Venuse Concierge Services is made.
