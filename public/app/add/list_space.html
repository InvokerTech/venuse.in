  • A security deposit should not replace event insurance
    A deposit can cover small damages and inconveniences (e.g. marks on the wall, leaving behind a mess, a blown out speaker), however you will still need to provide a form of insurance.
  • Determining your security deposit amount
    Many space owners request a security deposit equal to a small percentage of the total listing price. Event organizers are less likely to select spaces with high security deposits, as this amount must be paid in cash.
